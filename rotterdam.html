Rotterdam

Where to Eat

Dionysos

This restaurant is in the city center and if you like Greek food it’s somewhere that you don’t want to overlook. The menu is available online and when you visit it is also available in English. It’s a popular place to make sure you make a reservation online before visiting.

What Not To Miss

Baroeg Open Air

Usually held in September at Zuiderpark. Hosting 5 stages and 2 tents this festival caters to almost all music types and is definitely one not to miss. This is an all day festival and with tickets (currently) only being 15 euros is a great way to have a fun, cheap day out. Whilst there make sure you visit the market area, there’s all kinds of cool merchandise to buy from pin badges to clothing. For the adults you can grab a pint and a burger and obviously there’s soft drinks and snacks available for the little ones.

Where To Visit

Willemswerf

Often referred to as ‘Building N’ this office block is situated by the River Maas. Measuring 288 foot tall this building is well known not only for the famous shipping companies inside but also for Jackie Chan sliding down the side of it in the movie ‘Who Am I?’
