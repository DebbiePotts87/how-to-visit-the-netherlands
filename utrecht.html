Utrecht

Where to Eat

Hofman

Why not pop down to Hofman for a burger and some live entertainment. With everything from live music to comedy nights there is sure to be something on that you will enjoy. Hofman has an outdoor terrace which is open all year round so if you fancy your lunch in the sun or dinner in the rain they have it covered.

Have a look at the menu online but be sure to make a reservation before you visit because it gets very busy especially when live events are on. While you’re on the website booking your table, check out their live events calendar so you don’t book a cozy date night when they’re hosting a club night.

What Not To Miss

Winter Market

Located not to far from Utrecht centraal this festive market is held every December. Molen de Ster is transformed into a winter wonderland and holds around 50 stalls full of local produce and gifts for the festive season. Don’t worry about being cold, there’s stalls offering warm traditional Dutch food and drink to keep you going.

The winter market is only held for one weekend a year at the start of December so if you’re wanting to pay this beautiful market a visit then make sure you check out their website for the dates and times to plan your trip accordingly.

Where To Visit

Dom Tower

At 112 meters tall this church has the largest spire in The Netherlands. This iconic symbol of Utrecht had the first foundation stone laid in 1321 and took 60 years to complete! The first restoration was started in 1519, restoration occurs every 50ish years. Book a ticket and have a guided tour of this iconic symbol!

Dom Tower is centered in Dom Square, Dom Square was originally a fortress, there you will find the tourist center which offers a one hour tour of the tower. The tower is open to visitors 7 days a week between 10am and 5pm giving visitors plenty of opportunity to see this historical building.
