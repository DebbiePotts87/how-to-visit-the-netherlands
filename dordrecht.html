Dordrecht

Where to Eat

La Cubanita

All you can eat tapas! Friendly staff serving delicious, fresh tapas and the best part is that as long as you eat it, you can have as much as you want. The restaurant is beautifully decorated and has a cozy atmosphere. Unfortunately there is no option to sit outside and enjoy the views in the summer but if you like tapas it’s definitely worth a visit.

Located to the side of the river, La Cubanita has beautiful views if you walk there or if you’re driving there is on the road parking outside. Menus are available in both Dutch and English and a reservation is required.

What Not To Miss

Dordtevaar

Want to see the main sights of Dordrecht and have a guided tour? Take a tour of the warehouse, go under some beautiful bridges and sail past dark vistas on this electric boat. Be warned though, this is an open top boat so if the sky isn’t blue take a coat or umbrella just in case!

The Netherlands has the largest and most significant sea port in Europe (Port of Rotterdam) so it would only be right to sail down the river of the first city in The Netherlands and experience all it has to offer. The tour lasts just under an hour but the boat only holds up to 40 people so booking in advance is a must.

Where To Visit

Grote Kerk
(Big Church)

Building of the tower began in 1339, the intention was to make it the largest tower in The Netherlands, however due to the soft Dutch soil the tower began to lean, so in 1506 building was halted.

This church tower really is a sight to behold and if you want to you can pay a small fee to climb the 275 steps to the top. Be sure to check out the interior even if the climb to the top isn’t something you fancy, Grote Kerk boasts about having impressive monuments and chapels.
